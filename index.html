    MELAMINE Polish

    Melamine polish is a closed pore polish, which means it seals the pores of the wood, making it non-breathable and protecting it from hot and cold surfaces. Melamine polish is made up of a thin resin that when applied to a wood surface generates a thin film-like layer.

    PU wood Polish

    Polyurethane polish is used for protecting the wooden furniture from rust, weather, abrasion and other chemicals. The PU Polish protects the furniture from moisture, scratches, dust, water, UV rays, and weathering while still giving it a lovely appearance. Water-based and solvent-based PU Polishes are also available.

    Varnish Wood Polish

    Useful for bare and stained wooden items, varnish wood polish is available in many sheens, but can only be applied to wet areas.
